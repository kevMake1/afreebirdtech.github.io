Sponsor A Child

HOW CAN YOU SPONSOR A CHILD?

You can help children reach their dreams!
Your donations will contribute to education, supplies, transport,and much more!

When you donate to our Take Flight program, you are bringing strength

to our children and their families!

A Free Bird (EIN 26-2963000) is a 501(c)3 not-for-profit.

Under 501(c)3, donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent provided by law.








Meet Elijah

“Elijah loves taking piano lessons with Landon. Elijah is 15 and he constantly looks out the window when its near the time that Landon should be arriving. Elijah definitely wants to continue with piano lessons. Thank you for allowing my son the opportunity to do this. He mentioned in past conservations that he wanted to play the piano but we never took it serious. We are grateful for being part of a Free Bird.” --- Karon King-Blades (Elijah’s Mom)

Meet David

“David had a great experience to learn from such a talented ‘teacher Joe’. David is very grateful for the inspiration and guidance from his teacher which allowed him to write an original song entitled “We Are’. David was taught additional techniques such as ‘double picking’ or ‘scraping the pick’ which will continue to benefit David in his Music Journey. Thank you to the Free Bird Organization for such a wonderful experience for David to continue lessons on guitar. ”







Meet Isabella

"Isabella is continuing her passion in music! Thanks to our Take Flight program, Isabella has a new drum set and is learning from her tutor Miguel. Isabella has been practicing with Miguel for a couple of months and hopes to perform on stage soon!"
