More than 10,000 children in the United States under the age of 15 will be diagnosed with cancer or other chronic illnesses this year. While research shows the beneficial effects of . therapeutic arts on those battling cancer and other chronic illnesses, funding for arts education continues to be cut from schools and government programs. With your help, AFB can help more children reveal their exceptional gifts while giving them strength and comfort during the fight of their lives.

A Free Bird (EIN 26-2963000) is a 501(c)3 not-for-profit. Under 501(c)3, donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent provided by law.
