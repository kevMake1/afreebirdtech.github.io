The Lighthouse Rescue!
Saturday, July 27, 2019 05:00PM @ PIP'S Island 400 W 42nd Street, New York, NY 10036

In this hour-long interactive journey, kids lead the way through a magical island where they'll get to man a flying ship, design giant cakes, explore the deep sea, and more! Kids are free to move around from room to room as they join performers, puppets, and animated characters in a series of challenges that help the story unfold. Experience the groundbreaking show that families can't get enough of -- Winner of BEST FAMILY SHOW in New York!
