Get Excited



Get Excited allows local businesses and consumers the opportunity
to get involved and give back. A Free Bird is an IRS recognized 501(c)(3)
nonprofit organization and your donations qualify as a
charitable contribution. By donating, you also will gain
access to a community of like-minded people who are patrons of the arts
and supporters of our cause.



Benefits of Participation

-Have your business logo on our Get Excited platform, on our website
and in our AFB Magazine
-Your business will also be mentioned on our social channels and marketing
communications including our Facebook and Instagram pages and our monthly
newsletter.
-Your contribution will qualify as a charitable
contribution under the IRS.
