Sponsor a child

Would you like to sponsor a freebirds children? Here's how! Our sponsoring a child program has two options. You may either sponsor a child, which will pay for his/her Dream,Take Flight, and Free programs, or you may sponsor an entire arts education program. The cost for one child to go through all three of our programs is $2,762, but any onetime amount or monthly contribution is welcome. If you sponsor one child, you will have the option of being emailed updates about a child's progress. Our children thank you!
