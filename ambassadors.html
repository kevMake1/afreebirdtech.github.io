Ambassadors

Our ambassadors are extensions of A Free Bird that represent our organization throughout
the United States. Embodying the spirited energy of creativity, these individuals assist us in carrying
out our quest to help children affected by cancer through the arts. Our ambassadors epitomize
our organization’s mission and help us in our pursuit to expand nationwide.

ARE YOU QUALIFIED?

A Free Bird is always looking for motivated individuals to get involved with our organization.
Applicants for A Free Bird Ambassadors should meet the following requirements:

  • Involved and/or interested in the arts
  • 21 years of age or older
  • Lives within a major city of the United States
  • Must have a degree/diploma from an institution or university
