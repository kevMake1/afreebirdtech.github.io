I am A Free Bird


Calling to mind positivity and comfort, I Am A Free Bird combines the uplifting limetta leaf with refreshing green citrus notes, wet-aloe freshness, warm woods and soothing musks. This invigorating, unisex fragrance not only offers a comforting yet vibrant scent, it supports our work of bringing arts education to children undergoing cancer treatment. I Am a Free Bird is the scent equivalent to the “hope” that represents A Free Bird Organization.


Beyond its refreshing scent, each I Am A Free Bird fragrance bottle includes an amethyst gemstone necklace. The magnetic back on the birthstone allows it to attach to the fragrance cap or the chain of the necklace, channeling amethyst and magnetic healing properties, aligning with our mission to empower and artistically heal our partnered families.


I Am A Free Bird invokes the hope, dreams and energy of childhood. All proceeds will help support A Free Bird programs. Join Mia in the movement to artistically empower children in need.


I Am A Free Bird is launching in October 2020. Sign up here to be notified of I Am A Free Bird updates and availability!


Mia's Story


Mia joined A Free Bird with the dream of becoming a model. After overcoming a malignant brain tumor, she completed our Take Flight program twice. Mia was cast by fashion designer Janelle Funari to walk the runway at New York Fashion Week. She also learned to read sheet music with our tutor Jobelle. Being the face of I Am a Free Bird is the culmination of her growth and efforts, and a big step toward her dream.

Birthstone Necklace

I Am A Free Bird is not only a fragrance but a necklace, too!

Coming from Artigas in northern Uruguay, an area that’s internationally known for its beautiful gemstones, the first edition of I Am A Free Bird’s pendant is an amethyst stone. Amethyst has healing powers to help with physical ailments and emotional issues. The necklace is also a magnet, channeling magnetic healing properties.


Each year, A Free Bird will release a new edition of the fragrance, which will feature a new birthstone pendant.
